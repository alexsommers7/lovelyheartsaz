Who we are

Administering personalized, loving care for those in need is our passion. Our caretaker Eunice brings 12+ years of experience as a PCA, along with the compassion and empathy that your loved one deserves. Regardless of age or ability, all individuals love to be pampered and cared for, even if it's just for a few hours. We're based out of Scottsdale, Arizona, but we believe the best care happens where your loved one is most comfortable - home. We'll meet you there.

Meet Eunice

The Lovely Hearts Treatment

Whether it's a bed bath or a shower, we ensure that our patients enjoy a relaxing and comfortable personal cleansing experience, leaving them feeling rejuvinated and refreshed.

After showering or bathing, your loved one will not be leaving the bathroom with wet hair. Curling, combing, you name it! We'll leave their hair the way they love it.

After every bath, we will apply lotion and perform a light massage while doing so. This therapeutic experience leaves our patients with glowing, moisturized skin and relaxed muscles.

We believe making our clients feel comfortable in their own skin starts with a clean shave. That's why we provide beard trimming services, along with mustache trimming and whiskers on the women!

Upon request, we will change linens and replace them with fresh ones on every visit. This is a seemingly small, but rather important step in maintaining a clean and safe home environment.

A note on COVID-19

With COVID-19 cases on the rise in Arizona and across the globe, we understand that you may have concerns in the areas of cleanliness and safety. We want you to know that client safety is our number one priority, and we take our sanitation measures very seriously. This includes, but is not limited to, always wearing a mask, washing hands frequently, sanitizing our equipment between uses, and more.
